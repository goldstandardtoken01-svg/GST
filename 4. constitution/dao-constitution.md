GST DAO Constitution

Article I — Purpose
GST DAO exists to steward the Gold Standard Token ecosystem through transparent, community‑driven governance.

Article II — Membership
All GST holders are members of the DAO.

Article III — Governance Rights
Members may:
- Submit proposals  
- Vote on proposals  
- Allocate treasury funds  
- Elect working groups  

Article IV — Treasury
The treasury is controlled exclusively by governance.

Article V — Proposals
A valid proposal must include:
- Summary  
- Motivation  
- Implementation details  
- Requested resources  

Article VI — Voting
- 1 GST = 1 vote  
- Simple majority for standard proposals  
- 60% quorum for treasury proposals  

Article VII — Amendments
Any member may propose amendments to this constitution.
