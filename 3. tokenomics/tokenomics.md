GST Tokenomics

Supply
- Fixed supply: 100,000,000 GST  
- No inflation  
- No hidden mint functions  

Distribution Breakdown
- 60% Community Allocation  
  Airdrops, public distribution, incentives  
- 20% Ecosystem Growth  
  Grants, partnerships, development  
- 10% Treasury  
  Governance‑controlled  
- 10% Contributors  
  Transparent, vested allocations  

Vesting
Contributor allocations vest linearly over 24 months.

Utility
- Governance  
- Access to ecosystem tools  
- Community rewards  

Treasury Rules
- All spending requires governance approval  
- No multisig control without community oversight  

Sustainability
GST is designed for long‑term alignment, not short‑term speculation.
